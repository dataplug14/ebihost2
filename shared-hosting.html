What is Shared Hosting?

Shared hosting allows multiple websites to use the same physical server. It’s the most affordable way to get reliable, secure hosting for small to medium websites, blogs and startups.

Which plan should I start with?

Starter is perfect for new or small sites. Choose Premium for growing traffic or multiple sites, and Business for resource‑heavy projects. You can upgrade at any time without downtime.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. Plans can be changed instantly from your client area. Any unused time on your current plan is automatically credited to the new plan.

Will you migrate my website for free?

Absolutely. We will migrate one existing website from another cPanel host at no cost. For additional sites, contact support and we’ll provide a quick quote.

Do you provide free SSL certificates?

Yes. All plans include free Let’s Encrypt SSL with automatic installation and renewal so your site is always served over HTTPS.

Which control panel do you use?

We use the latest cPanel for easy management of files, email, databases, backups and one‑click app installs.

Are backups included?

Yes. We take automatic weekly off‑site backups. Need a restore? Open a ticket and our team will assist. We still recommend keeping your own copies for extra safety.

What uptime do you guarantee?

Our shared platform targets 99.95% uptime with 24/7 monitoring. If we ever fall short, we’ll make it right in line with our SLA.

Where are your servers located?

We operate in multiple regions so you can host close to your audience. Choose a location during signup or ask support to move you later—free of charge.

Is email hosting included?

Yes. You can create mailboxes through cPanel with IMAP/POP3/SMTP access and WebMail. Need advanced features? Check out our Professional Email.

Is a domain name included?

Domains are purchased separately. You can register a new domain or transfer an existing one to us.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept major debit/credit cards and PayPal. If you prefer another method, contact us and we’ll help.
